Our crew

Our crew members are dedicated professionals who are passionate about providing exceptional service and ensuring your safety. Experience the warm Filipino hospitality as our friendly crew, always ready to assist you, makes your travel experience truly memorable.




Cebu Pacific offers various services to enhance the travel experience.


Flight and Seat Selection

Passengers can select their preferred seats, including options for extra legroom and premium seating, during the booking process.

Baggage Services

The airline provides various baggage options, including additional allowances and special handling services, with the convenience of web check-in.

In-Flight Meals and Refreshments

Cebu Pacific offers a variety of pre-ordered and onboard purchase meal and snack options to cater to different tastes and dietary needs.







Our fleet

Cebu Pacific operates one of the youngest fleets in the world with an average age of 5 years as of end of 2019. CEB finished 2019 with 75 aircraft, comprised of eight Airbus A330CEO (Current Engine Option), 30 Airbus A320CEO, seven A321CEO, five Airbus A321NEO (New Engine Option), four Airbus A320NEO, 13 ATR 72-600, seven ATR 72-500, and one ATR Freighter.
